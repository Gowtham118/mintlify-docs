## Core Contracts

We have deployed two core contracts that power our platform:

1. **Create2ForwarderFactory**: Handles the creation and management of forwarding contracts
2. **Liquidity Router**: Manages liquidity paths and token swaps across various sources

## Deployment Addresses

Our contracts are deployed across multiple EVM-compatible networks to ensure wide coverage and accessibility.
